MAHILA MANDAL (LADIES WING)

The SCLP Samaj Ladies Wing operates under the banner of Shree Cutchi Leva Patel Samaj-Nairobi. It was revamped in April 2016.

The Ladies Wing organizes activities related to and involving women in our community with the following objectives:

  • To promote activities for enhancement of all types of skills for women of our community
  • To enhance educational, social, moral, physical and cultural advancement of women
  • To undertake programs for the relief of illiteracy, poverty and assistance to the needy

SCLP Samaj Ladies Wing carries out regular activities and events that mainly revolve around health, sports, Hindu festivals, team building, mental health, cooking, cultural dance, music, education, outdoor activities and charity.
